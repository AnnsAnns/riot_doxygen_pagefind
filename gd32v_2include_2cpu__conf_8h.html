Possible values of the SWJ_CFG field in the AFIO->PCF0 register.

Enumerator
SWJ_CFG_FULL_JTAG 

Full JTAG interface (reset value)

SWJ_CFG_NO_NJTRST 

JTAG enabled, but NJTRST disabled and pin PB4 usable as GPIO.

SWJ_CFG_NO_JTAG 

JTAG disabled, all debug pins usable as GPIOs.
